# Agent Orchestration

A security first platform for governed, identity aware AI agent execution. It is a distinct Goji Labs product and does not depend on the DeFi product family.

## What it is

A centralized control plane that orchestrates autonomous AI agents with the governance, identity enforcement, and audit that regulated enterprises require, closing the gap between agent capability and operational trust.

## The intelligence layer

The intelligence layer is an addition to the orchestration layer. Together they form the stronger SaaS product. The orchestration layer provides governed, identity aware execution. The intelligence layer adds the reasoning and decision capability on top of that controlled substrate. Sold together, they cover both the trust and control story that security teams require and the capability story that the business wants.
